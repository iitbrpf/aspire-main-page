Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Thank you for visiting the IITB Research Park website. At IITB Research Park, we understand the importance of protecting your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us through our website.

When you visit our website, we may collect personal information such as your name, gender, email address, phone number, personal address, and any other information only after you agree to provide it through contact forms or inquiries.

We use the personal information you provide to respond to your inquiries, provide requested services, and communicate with you about programs, events, and activities. Your information will not be shared without your consent unless required by law.

We may use cookies and similar technologies to analyze website traffic and enhance your browsing experience. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies; however, this may affect some website functionality.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those websites, and we are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing any information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at: rpoffice@iitbresearchpark.com

By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. Thank you for trusting IITB Research Park.
